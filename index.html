FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We've got answers to your questions

Get plenty of sleep the night before, eat a good meal within 4 hours of your appointment, avoid alcohol 24 hours prior, wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed, and stay hydrated. If it's your first tattoo, avoid caffeine on the day for those who get jittery easily.

Pricing depends on the size, complexity, placement on the body, and artist selected. Small tattoos start around Br 3,000 - Br 5,000, medium pieces range from Br 8,000 - Br 15,000, while large custom work or sleeves are priced by the session (Br 5,000 - Br 10,000 per hour). During your consultation, we'll provide a clear estimate after discussing your specific design.

Pain levels vary depending on your personal tolerance, tattoo placement, and duration. Most describe it as an intense scratching or vibration. Bony areas (ribs, hands, feet) tend to be more sensitive, while meatier areas (upper arm, thigh) are more comfortable. Our artists work carefully to minimize discomfort and can provide numbing options when appropriate.

Keep the initial bandage on for 2-4 hours. Wash gently with lukewarm water and mild fragrance-free soap, pat dry with a clean paper towel, and apply a thin layer of recommended ointment 2-3 times daily. Avoid soaking, swimming, direct sunlight, and gym sessions for 2 weeks. Wear loose clothing over the area. Full healing takes about 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed aftercare instructions specific to your tattoo.

You must be at least 18 years old to get tattooed at our studio without exceptions. We strictly comply with all health regulations and require valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or Aadhar card with photo) as proof of age before any tattoo service. We do not tattoo minors, even with parental consent.
